**Front:** What are the Z critical values for common statistical power levels?

**Back:** The Z critical values are:

- For power = 0.80, \( Z = 0.84 \)
- For power = 0.90, \( Z = 1.28 \)
- For power = 0.95, \( Z = 1.64 \)
