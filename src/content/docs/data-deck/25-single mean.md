**Front:** What is the [[variance]] formula for a single mean?

**Back:** The variance for a single mean is:
\[ \sigma^2 = \frac{\sum\_{i=1}^k (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{k - 1} \]
Where:

- \( x_i \) are the data points.
- \( \bar{x} \) is the sample mean.
- \( k \) is the sample size.
