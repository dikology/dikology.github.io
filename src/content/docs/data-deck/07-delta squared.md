**Front:** How do you calculate Delta squared for a proportion?

**Back:** Delta squared is calculated as:
\[ \Delta^2 = (P_2 - P_1)^2 \]
Where:

- \( P_1 \) is the baseline proportion.
- \( P_2 \) is the treatment proportion.
