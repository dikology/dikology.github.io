**Front:** What are the Z critical values for common significance levels (alpha) in a two-tailed test?

**Back:** The Z critical values are:

- For alpha = 0.01, \( Z = 2.58 \)
- For alpha = 0.05, \( Z = 1.96 \)
- For alpha = 0.10, \( Z = 1.64 \)
