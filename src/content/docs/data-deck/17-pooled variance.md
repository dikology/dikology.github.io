**Front:** What is the pooled variance in a two-sample case for proportions?

**Back:** The pooled variance is:
\[ \sigma^2 = P_2(1 - P_2) + P_1(1 - P_1) \]
Where:

- \( P_1 \) is the proportion for group 1.
- \( P_2 \) is the proportion for group 2.
